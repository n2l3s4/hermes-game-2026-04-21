# Bobo's Shape Safari 🦁🐘🍎🧀

A fun shape-matching safari game for Bobo! Help feed the hungry jungle animals by dragging the right shaped food to each one.

## Play the Game

**🎮 [Play Now](https://n2l3s4.github.io/hermes-game-2026-04-21/)**

## How to Play

- Look at the shape on each animal's belly
- Drag the matching shaped food from the bottom tray to the animal
- Feed all 5 animals to win!
- Listen for the happy sounds when you get it right

## Animals to Feed

- 🦁 Lion loves the 🍎 **Circle Apple**
- 🐘 Elephant loves the 🧀 **Square Cheese**
- 🐻 Bear loves the 🍪 **Star Cookie**
- 🐰 Bunny loves the 🥕 **Triangle Carrot**
- 🐵 Monkey loves the 🍓 **Heart Strawberry**

- Simple, colorful design perfect for 5-year-olds
